What's on the glass

Everything your morning needs, at a glance

Each panel is optional — switch anything off and the glass stays clean and black.

Clock & greeting

A bold clock, the date and week number, and a greeting with your name — in Swedish or English, 24h or 12h.

Weather & forecast

Live local weather with a multi-day forecast. Uses your location, or any place you type in.

Live stock charts

A full-width chart in the centre, or a dedicated Stock mode with up to five tickers — day, week, month, year or all-time.

Bus departures

Live departures from your nearest stop, automatically — or pin an exact stop if you prefer.

Electricity spot price

Swedish hourly spot prices for your bidding area (SE1–SE4), so you know when power is cheap.

Motion wake

The screen sleeps to black and wakes when someone walks up, using the front camera — processed on the device, never stored or sent anywhere.

Phone remote

Change settings from your phone and push them to the mirror with a shared Mirror ID — no ladder required.

Business mode

Show your own images or a full-screen PDF instead — menus, offers, opening hours. A shop window on glass.

Make it yours

Six accent colours, rotating one-liners in Swedish or English, your own centre image or GIF — even an eye screensaver.

How it works

From spare tablet to smart mirror in three steps

Open the app

Visit the mirror page in the tablet's browser and add it to the home screen. That's the whole install.

Make it yours

Pick your location, language and panels in the settings. Choose an accent colour. Press Start mirror.

Hang it up

Prop the tablet on a shelf, or mount it behind two-way mirror glass for the full effect. Plug it in and forget it.

Private by design

Your mirror. Your data. Your device.

reflekt is a single web page. There is no account, no sign-up and no server holding your settings.

  • Everything stays on the tablet. Settings, images and preferences live in the browser — nowhere else.
  • Motion detection is local. Camera frames are analysed on-device and immediately discarded.
  • No tracking, no ads. The page fetches weather, transit and prices — and nothing about you.
