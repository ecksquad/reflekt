Motion wake
Uses the front camera to detect movement and wake the screen; it sleeps to black when no one is around. Frames are processed on-device and never stored or sent anywhere.
Stay awake for
How long the screen stays on after the last movement.
Motion sensitivity
How much movement wakes the screen. High trips easily; Low needs bigger movement.
